Our Story

Every 30 minutes, someone is diagnosed with chronic kidney disease in the United States. That's over 37 million Americans currently living with CKD — many of whom don't know it until the disease is already in its advanced stages. Kidney disease is the 9th leading cause of death in the U.S., and dialysis is grueling, expensive, and not a cure.

💡 Over 800,000 Americans are on dialysis or living with a kidney transplant. Without better research, this number will continue to climb.

Our team has developed a non-invasive biomarker screening method that could identify CKD up to 8 years earlier than current diagnostic tools. We are raising funds to complete Phase 2 clinical trials. Your contribution goes toward:

🔬 Lab testing & specimen analysis
🏥 Hospital partnership fees
💻 AI-assisted diagnostic modeling
📚 Publishing & peer review

We've already received matching grants, meaning every dollar you give is amplified. We need to reach $85,000 through public donations to unlock those funds.

No one should lose a loved one to a disease that could have been caught earlier. Our lead researcher, Dr. Amara Mensah, lost her mother to stage 5 CKD when she was 19. That loss fueled a career dedicated to making sure others don't face the same grief.

We are a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit. 100% of net proceeds go toward research. Our financials are publicly audited annually and we provide quarterly updates to every donor.

Thank you for believing that better science leads to better lives. Share this page — even a single share can change the trajectory of this research.
